The truth about money at 25
You don't need income to build financial habits. Every no-spend day is money saved. Every job application is a potential income stream. Every $1 kept today is worth $7+ by 40 if invested. You're not behind — you're building the foundation most people skip.

Debt payoff strategy
Avalanche method: Pay minimums on all debts. Put every extra dollar toward the highest-interest debt first. This saves the most money. Once one is gone, roll that payment into the next. This is the math-optimal path to being debt-free.

The millionaire's first move isn't investing
It's income. You cannot invest what you don't have. Aim for 5+ job applications per day, minimum. While job hunting: list anything on Facebook Marketplace, do gig work (Instacart, DoorDash, TaskRabbit), or offer a skill on Fiverr. Every dollar in reduces pressure and builds the habit of earning.
